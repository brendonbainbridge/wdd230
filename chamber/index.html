Rexburg City, Idaho

Rexburg is an amazing and safe community. This city is growing quickly and with the help of having the University called BYUI in its city limits, it greatly helps the businesses all around by bringing in all new students every semester. It also allows the city to keep growing as well as having the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day saints Ideals and teaching be a strong factor in its culture. What this means is that Rexburg is one of the safest cities in America, and a great place to move here regardless if you are a student looking to come to school, or wanting to move you or your family. There are many local businesses as well as many activities that can help you join in on the fun, and fit right in.

Facts about Rexburg and its history

  • Rexburg was born from Pioneers that were members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
  • BYUI is a University that is in the city of Rexburg, and is owned by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
  • Rexburg has a great schooling system in place, and has made efforts to expand and improve what they already have.
  • Rexburg is ranked as one of the safest places in the America to live in.
  • Every Semester at BYUI thousands of students come in for the semester.
  • Its illegal to throw a snowball in the City of Rexburg
  • The lowest temperature Rexburg experienced was -36F in February 1, 1985.
  • Due to the culture of the Church in this city, the amount of Soda Shops here higher.
