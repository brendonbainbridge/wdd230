WDD230 Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

This website will outline the plan for the Chamber of Commerce Site that is being put together as a class assignment. Below you will find the outlined details

Site Name

The name that was picked out for the website was “Rexburg Idaho Chamber of Commerce” The reason why is because the website would be based on a local community and wanted to have the purpose of the website to be clearly visible by just the title. Having a title like this is also short, clear and presentable, especially in a business sense, which is one of the main target audiences for this website as well.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help benefit the community, and this can be done by helping those looking to join the community as well as informing them about local events. Another way this would be helpful would be to bring to light the local businesses that are in Rexburg, which would also help the community as a whole financially as well.

Scenarios

Questions that may be asked by the website representing the target audience:
• What are local businesses that are a must to go check out?
• How does the school BYUI create and affect the community as well as its businesses?
• What is the contact information for the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce?
• What is the benefit of becoming a part of the Chamber of Commerce as a business?
• How can my business become a part of the Chamber of Commerce?
• Where and what are good times to network and grow with the community?
• What are community events I should go to?
